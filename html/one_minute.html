One Minute Films

Brown University ~ Brown Motion Pictures

Starting freshman year of college, Brown Motion Pictures, a prominenet club on campus, began running one-minute film competitions that my friends and I entered in to. We come in first place each year with our film being screened in front of 300 people at a local cinema house. These films are a ton of fun to make - they allow me to develop my camera skills, apply my knowledge of theatrical lighting to film, and direct my actor friends.
