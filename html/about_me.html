Jack Dermer

Creativity exists in the intersection of many different fields. My unconventional background in both the arts and computer science allows me to be a creative problem-solver. Whether with a line in a script or a line of code, I strive to balance both the technical and aesthetic nature of a given project.

I am a junior at Brown studying Computer Science and Psychology. Within CS, I specialize in Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence. In Psychology, I work in a developmental lab studying how children learn and collaborate. Outside of class, I am a lighting designer for several dance companies on campus as well as a filmmaker.
